*   **Title:** Training the Public on Anonymous Reporting

    2.  **Provide Details:** "Emphasize that they should provide as much detail as possible, but only what they are comfortable with. Mention the file upload feature for evidence."
    3.  **Optional Contact Info:** "Reassure users that this is completely optional and that they can remain anonymous."
